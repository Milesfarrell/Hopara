Camper Return

We expect the camper to be dropped back to us by a 'reasonable hour'. This will be decided on pick up as it revolves around other rentals. We are flexible, however there will be a late fee if you drop the camper back at a time that will affect other customers.

Insurance Information

Insurance can be purchased for an additional $10 per day. The excess for the insurance is priced for certain age groups and types of licenses. Our insurance gives you an extensive range of cover provided you act within the law.
