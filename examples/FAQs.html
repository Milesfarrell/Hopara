How many can the camper sleep?

The Camper has room for 2 humans. However, other tenting equipment can be rented so more crew can join on the adventure.

See more

What cooking arrangements does the camper have?

The Camper has 2 burner gas stove, running water with a sink, electrical outlets for other cooking equipment, crockery for two people and basic cooking essentials provided by us. Luxury as.

See more

What security does the camper have?

All compartments are easily lockable, and we provide ball locks & wheel clamps for your peace of mind. Our team will give you a safety and security run down before your adventure starts to ensure you feel confident and safe taking the camper away.

Can the Camper be taken off-road?

The insurance provided for the camper does not cover off-roading adventures so the camper is not fit for these missions. Please see the rental terms and conditions or get in contact with one of our staff if you want to know more.

Is linen and sleeping equipment provided?

No, we would like you to provide your own sleeping equipment. Something as simple as a bottom sheet and a sleeping bag will suffice. However, if you do need some sleeping equipment, click on the ‘add-ons’ page and see what we can offer

See more

Is the camper self-contained?

Unfortunately no, this camper does not have a toilet. However, we can recommend adventures where toilets are easily accessible and we give you a roll of TP for good measure at the start of your trip.

Does the camper have roof racks?

Yes 100%. We recommend making use of these to bring your toys on the mission too. We can give you a hand using tie downs as well.

What insurance do I need?

We have you covered. See contact tab for details, however upon hire we will give you the rundown of what insurance we provide, at what cost and how we operate in that regard

What other equipment will I need?

What we recommend is:
Bed linen, sleeping bags, food & water for your trip, warm clothes, adventure equipment, and sunscreen.

Do I need a tow bar on my car?

Yep. That’s the main thing we need you to have, the rest we can help you with! Any car with a tow bar should be able to drag this light wee thing.

Can you teach me how to back this thing?

Of course. We have taught some very unconfident backers to back this little beauty with a few easy bits of advice. However, if you do find yourself in a tricky spot, because the camper is so light, we recommend removing the camper from the tow bar and moving it manually. A bit of mahi eh

How much does it weigh?

Without any equipment in it, only 500kg. A whole lot lighter compared to other bulky units on the market. This helps with fuel economy, and easy manoeuvring
